Promotion Producer (2019-59)
Full Time
South Florida's #1 News Station, WSVN 7 is looking for a Promotion Producer to join our Creative Services team. This is not an entry level position. Must have experience writing, producing and editing image spots, news teases and commercials for clients. College degree preferred with skills in Adobe Premiere, Final Cut, Photoshop and After Effects. Please include your resume and a link to your demo reel with your application.
Maintenance Worker - PT (2020-6)
Part Time
We are looking for a part time maintenance worker to assist with building repairs & maintenance. Ideal candidate will have basic knowledge of HVAC, plumbing, electrical, carpentry among other technical skills. Position requires physical demands to exert up to 70 lbs. of force occasionally, and/or up to 50 lbs. of force frequently, and/or up to 20 lbs. of force constantly to move objects. Other physical requirements of the job include the ability to bend/crouch/or kneel, see, reach, climb and balance. Position will also require individual run errands. Individual must have a valid driver's license with a clean record, and must have knowledge of basic computer skills. Minimum High School Diploma required.
Promotions Coordinator (2020-13)
Part Time
Candidate will handle scheduling of on-air promotional spots and various office duties. Individual will also have the opportunity to write, edit and produce news promos, updates and sales projects. Strong organizational skills and knowledge of Adobe Premiere, Final Cut and Photoshop required. College degree preferred.
Photographer (2020-24)
Full Time
We are looking for a very aggressive photographer with good story telling abilities. Must have a good "news eye" for our fast paced news shows, be able to work under pressure, meet deadlines, as well as work well with others. Knowledge of Sony HD cameras and non-linear editing is a must. A minimum of 2-3 years experience and a valid class "D" drivers license is required.
DECO Cyber/Journalist (2020-28)
Part Time
Selected Candidate will assist The Cyber-Journalist/Producer in writing and entering data into the WSVN-TV website to make sure it has the latest news posted. Candidate must have excellent writing & grammar skills and be proficient in Windows 10. Applicant should also have a strong knowledge of Internet sites, and possess good web researching skills.

Producer Associate - Niteside (2020-51)
Part Time
Looking for someone who is proactive, energetic and willing to work hard to get on the producing track. Must possess strong writing skills with vo's, vosot's and packages and isn't afraid to jump in on breaking news, assisting producers in grabbing video... logging sound... and helping to turn content quickly.
Deco Drive Managing Editor (2020-54)
Full Time
Deco Drive, the number one entertainment show in Access and Late Night, is searching for a Managing Editor. This candidate will work closely with the Executive Producer on this funny, irreverent South Florida live show. The Managing Editor will help oversee staff, schedule shoots, approve content, and communicate with newsroom management. Deco is looking for someone with a keen sense of humor, strong writing skills, and ability to work well with others.
Segment DECO Producer (2020-57)
Full Time
Responsible for finding, writing and producing entertainment and lifestyle packages and news stories for daily half-hour news program. Candidate should be a self-motivator, able to generate unique stories with a quick turn-around for air. Must be able to wear two hats: one of a news producer with a basic grasp of current events and also an entertainment producer who is aware of what's popular in show business and pop culture. Two years minimum news writing experience preferred. Should be able to work nights and weekends when necessary.
Writer (2021-6)
Part Time
Writes copy for newscasts and assists producers in the production of shows. This is an entry-level position, but applicants must have solid broadcast writing skills, be willing to work all hours, and want to learn the business from the ground up. Must be proficient in Windows 10. Our writers are considered for future producer openings, so those with a strong interest in producing should apply.
Reporter (2021-7)
Full Time
Develops, researches and writes stories for broadcast on the air. Bachelor's degree in journalism required. Minimum 2-3 years experience as a television news reporter required. Looking for someone who is skilled at covering live, breaking news, and who possesses a creativity that sets them apart. Submit a link to your reel along with your application and resume.
News Maintenance Technician (2021-8)
Full Time
Must have knowledge of troubleshooting electronic equipment and be mechanically inclined. Previous TV ENG experience preferred, knowledge of microwave, satellite, and RF are a plus. Must be proficient in IT, Sony HD Camera, Sony decks , Cisco Switches , experience in different format of video streaming, bonded cellular ( LIVEU), audio and video signal distribution.
Chyron Operator (2021-10)
